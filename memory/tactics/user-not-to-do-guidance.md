# User Not-To-Do Guidance

## Purpose
Define how Plenra communicates prohibited or risky user actions after a decision.

## Output Examples
- do not stop the campaign immediately
- do not raise budget under panic
- do not change multiple variables at once
- do not conclude from a single day of data

## Rules
- guidance must be tied to actual decision context
- guidance must be specific
- guidance must reduce harmful action, not increase confusion
